About Me


Currently working as an Auto Damage Adjuster for Geico Insurance, I have enrolled in the Coding Bootcamp offered by The University of North Carolina at Charlotte in the hopes that I may develop the skills that will allow me to transfer to an internal division within my company. We are renowned within the auto insurance industry for our use of technology to aid not only our customers but our employees as well. My goal upon completion of this course is to obtain a position where I can use the skills learned through this course to develop technologies that will be used by other adjusters like myself.


My interests outside of work range from cooking and video games to hiking and snowboarding. It is my dream to relocate to Colorado where I can spend more of my time in the mountains and pursuing my interests and hobbies.
